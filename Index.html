Artist of the Year Award

Every summer time, the announcement of the Artist of the Year Award is held on this website. The nominees for this award incorporate artists from all over the world. However, only three of them will be included in the final round. The winner of the final round will be determined by the most votes received within the given period. However, to build suspense, the names of the nominees won't be shown, instead only the first letter of their names will be revealed.
